This is a demopage for the service we build during 4 weeks at #osoc20. Our service enriches data coming from our client FRIS, which is a search portal for all publicly funded Flemish research (FRIS portal).

When you select a project or a publication in the dropdown, you will see data appear, this is the original data coming from our client FRIS that they want to enrich.

When you press the 'get keywords' button. This data will be send through our service, the enriched data will be presented. This enriched data contains keywords that are generated from the project/publications abstracts. The keywords will be used to have a better searching experience on the FRIS portal.
